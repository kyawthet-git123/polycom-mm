ELV Solutions

Extra-Low Voltage Infrastructure Excellence

Grid Power Connection & Outdoor Cable Infrastructure Setup

Our ELV engineering team managing the deployment of main grid power inputs and outdoor signal cabling lines. Using certified scaffolding and strict safety protocols, we ensure secure, weather-resistant structural cabling connectivity from external utility poles directly into the facility's main server infrastructure.

Rooftop Solar PV Mounting Structure Installation

Integrating sustainable energy solutions into critical power backup systems. This stage showcases the precision alignment and installation of heavy-duty aluminum mounting rails on the facility’s rooftop, structurally engineered to support solar PV panels for auxiliary green power generation.

High-Capacity Battery Bank & Industrial Power Backup Setup

Ensuring zero downtime during grid power transitions. Our engineers are seen deploying and structurally anchoring multi-tiered, high-capacity Lithium-ion battery banks. This includes the precise serial linking of battery modules using heavy-duty jumper busbars to handle continuous enterprise power demands.

Overhead Cable Tray & Containment Trunking Routing

Establishing clean and safe pathways for power and data lines. We design and install high-level overhead cable trays and specialized containment trunking (red routing enclosures) to segregate high-voltage lines from sensitive network signal cables, preventing interference and organizing facility-wide routing.

APC Smart Cabinet Integration & Premium Cable Dressing

Precision execution inside the heart of the server room. Our team installs critical network switches, termination modules, and fiber optics within premium APC (by Schneider Electric) Smart Cabinets. Every single blue UTP LAN cable and yellow fiber strand is meticulously combed, bundled, and bound using premium Velcro tape to optimize airflow, cooling efficiency, and future maintenance troubleshooting.

Advanced Network Testing, Live UPS Commissioning & Handover

The final validation phase of our infrastructure deployment. Our engineers perform rigorous end-to-end network signal testing using industry-standard Fluke Testers to certify cable integrity. This phase culminates in the live commissioning of the massive APC Smart-UPS multi-cabinet backup system, ensuring flawless operational stability, load balancing, and status monitoring before formal project handover.
