ICT Solutions

Transforming your business with cutting-edge Information and Communication Technology

Infrastructure Solutions

Building the resilient physical foundation for modern enterprise operations.

  • Data Center Design & Build: Comprehensive design, hot/cold aisle containment, cooling management, and clean agent fire suppression systems for high-density server rooms.
  • Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) & Battery Energy Storage: High-availability industrial UPS systems and advanced Lithium-ion battery banks deployed to ensure zero-downtime operations during power anomalies.
  • Structured Cabling & Containment Systems: Precision fiber optic splicing, multi-pair copper LAN cabling, and overhead/underfloor cable tray routing with strict adherence to premium cable dressing standards.
  • Smart Solar Power Integration: Seamless pairing of hybrid solar PV energy grids with core enterprise power infrastructure to reduce operational carbon footprint and energy costs.

Network Solutions

Connecting your workforce and operations with fast, seamless, and secure architecture.

  • Enterprise Routing & Switching: Deployment of robust core, distribution, and access switches alongside high-throughput routers to handle complex multi-VLAN data traffic.
  • Enterprise Wireless Network Infrastructure: High-density wireless access points and centralized wireless controllers designed for flawless indoor/outdoor roaming and secure guest access management.
  • SD-WAN & Multi-Site Connectivity: Scalable Software-Defined Wide Area Network (SD-WAN) and secure site-to-site VPN solutions to interlink headquarters, branches, and remote factories efficiently.

System Solutions

Optimizing server environments, critical business applications, and data lifecycle management.

  • Compute & Enterprise Storage Architecture: High-performance rack/tower servers paired with high-capacity Centralized Storage Arrays (SAN/NAS) engineered for intensive write/read workloads.
  • Virtualization & Cloud Computing: Hypervisor deployment and bare-metal virtualization to consolidate physical hardware resources, improve server efficiency, and enable hybrid cloud readiness.
  • Enterprise Backup & Disaster Recovery (DR): Automated, offsite, and immutable backup systems with defined RTO (Recovery Time Objective) and RPO (Recovery Point Objective) to guarantee business continuity.

Security Solutions

Defending your digital assets, networks, and endpoints against sophisticated cyber threats.

  • Next-Generation Firewall (NGFW): Perimeter defense systems equipped with deep packet inspection, intrusion prevention systems (IPS), and application-level control to block advanced network threats.
  • Endpoint Security & Extended Detection (EDR): AI-driven host protection for corporate laptops, desktops, and mobile devices to isolate and neutralize malware, phishing variants, and ransomware threats.
  • SIEM (Security Information and Event Management): 24/7 centralized log correlation and security event analysis engine that detects behavioral anomalies, visualizes threats, and automates incident response.
  • Email & Web Security Gateways: Cloud or on-premise inspection engines that filter malicious web traffic, block spear-phishing attempts, and prevent organizational data leakage (DLP).

Hardware & Device Procurement

Your trusted single-source supplier for certified, enterprise-grade hardware.

  • Network Equipment: High-performance Routers, Managed Layer 2/3 Switches, Wireless Access Points, PoE Injectors, and Industrial Network Racks/Cabinets.
  • Security Appliances: Hardware-based Next-Gen Firewalls, VPN Gateways, and Network Access Control (NAC) appliances.
  • Enterprise Compute & Power: Rack/Tower Servers, SAN/NAS Storage Arrays, Enterprise Hard Drives (HDD/SSD), Smart-UPS Systems, and High-Capacity Battery Packs.
  • Workplace & Collaboration Devices: Business-class Laptops, Desktop Workstations, IP Phones, and Integrated Video Conferencing Systems.

Software & License Management

Providing official, fully compliant software licensing and lifecycle support.

  • Operating System (OS) Licenses: Official licensing models for both Enterprise Server Platforms and End-User Computing Environments.
  • Productivity & Cloud Collaboration Suites: Corporate cloud email hosting, team collaboration platforms, and office application licensing.
  • Cybersecurity & Endpoint Licenses: Subscription and perpetual licensing for Antivirus, EDR, Firewall Features, and SIEM platforms.
  • Virtualization & Business Continuity Licenses: Licenses for Virtual Machine (VM) management, data backup replication software, and database environments.
